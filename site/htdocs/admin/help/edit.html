Article Editor

Section

The section this article will be placed under..

Title

The title of the article.

Template

The template used to present this article.

Body

The content of your article. There are a number of tags you can use to markup your article text.

Release date

The date that your article is released to the world. Your article will only appear in menus after this date and before the expiry date. If you don't enter a release date it will default to the current date.

Note: Unreleased articles can be accessed via a direct URL to the article.

Expire date

The date from which you don't want the article to appear on your site.

Note: Expired articles can be found through the search engine.

Summary length

The length of the summary displayed for child articles by the <:summary:> tag.

Display threshold

This is used by the <:if UnderThreshold :> conditional tag. If the number of children is less than this value the condition is true.

Typically your templates will embed child articles if there are fewer than this number of child articles, otherwise a menu of child articles will be displayed.

Keywords

This is a comma-separated list of extra keywords used by the search engine.

List article

Choose how the article is listed in the sidebar menus, or in lists of child articles in the parent.

Thumbnail image

An image that can be used with the <:thumbnail:> tag to display a small image for the article in menus.

Files

Files attached to the article. This isn't available until the article has been created.

Uploaded images

Images attached to the article.
