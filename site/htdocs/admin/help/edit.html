Article Editor

Section

The section this article will be placed under.

Articles can be moved to other sections by selecting a different parent section from this menu.

Title

The title of the article. (The ‘title’ is indexed by the built-in search engine).

Title image

The title image will replace the text title <:title:> with an image file.

Title images need to be uploaded into the ‘/images/titles’ directory, as yet there is no admin interface to do this (this feature is rarely used).

Template

The HTML template used to layout the display of this article.

Body

The content of your article. There are a number of tags you can use to markup your article text. (The ‘body’ is indexed by the built-in search engine).

Page Title

Alternate page title. If this is set it is used for the page's <title> tag.

Author

Author of the content.

Release date

The date that your article is released to the world. Your article will only appear in menus after this date and before the expiry date. If you don't enter a release date it will default to the current date.

Note: Unreleased articles can be accessed via a direct URL to the article.

Expiry date

The date from which you don't want the article to appear on your site.

Note: Expired articles can be found through the built-in search engine.

Summary length

The length of the summary displayed for child articles by the <:summary:> tag.

Display threshold

This is used by the <:if UnderThreshold :> conditional tag. If the number of children is less than this value the condition is true.

Typically your templates will embed child articles if there are fewer than this number of child articles, otherwise a menu of child articles will be displayed.

Keywords

This is a comma-separated list of extra keywords used by the built-in search engine.

Keywords only need be explicitly indicated here if this article should be indexed under words not already used in the ‘title’ or ‘body’ of this article.

List article

Choose how the article is listed in the sidebar menus, or in lists of child articles in the parent.

Thumbnail image

An image that can be used with the <:thumbnail:> tag to display a small image for the article in menus.

To upload a ‘thumbnail’ image, either enter the name of an image on your computer, or use the Browse button to select an image file (Usually with a .GIF, .JPG, .PNG file extension) then remember to update the article or it wont be uploaded and saved.

Files

Files attached to the product. This isn't available until the product has been created.

You can upload and modify the files by clicking the "Manage Files" link.

Images

Images attached to the product. This isn't available until the product has been created.

You can upload and modify the images by clicking the "Manage Images" link.
