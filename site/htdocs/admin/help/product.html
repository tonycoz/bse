Product Editor

Title

The name of the product (NB: the title cannot be changed once an order has been placed that includes this product).

Summary

The extended name of the product (NB: the summary cannot be changed once an order has been placed that includes this product).

Catalog

The catalog in which this product should be listed.

Body

The content of your article. There are a number of tags you can use to markup your article text.

Lead time

The time required to prepare this product for shipping.

Retail price

The total cost of the product to the customer.

Wholesale price

The price this product costs to wholesalers.

GST

The Goods and Services Tax on this product (in dollars).

Release date

The date that your product is released to the world. Your product will only appear in menus after this date and before the expiry date. If you don't enter a release date it will default to the current date.

Note: Unreleased products can be accessed via a direct URL to the product.

Expiry date

The date from which you don't want the product to appear on your site.

Note: Expired products can be found through the search engine.

Summary length

The length of the summary displayed for products by the <:summary:> tag (typically this is only seen when the product is a stepkid of a non-shop section).

Display threshold

This is used by the <:if UnderThreshold :> conditional tag. If the number of children is less than this value the condition is true.

Typically your templates will embed child articles if there are fewer than this number of child articles, otherwise a menu of child articles will be displayed (typically this is only seen when the product is a stepkid of a non-shop section.

Options

Options available with this product, like sizes and colors (the options are set in Constants.pm).

Thumbnail image

An image that can be used with the <:thumbnail:> tag to display a small image for the product in menus.

Files

Files attached to the product. This isn't available until the product has been created.

Uploaded images

Images attached to the product.
